GTU CSE 495 - Final Project1

One of the problems with animals is that they take a wrong approach in the process of their capture. Primitive traps are still in use in most areas for capture. Classic traps are designed to capture the animal only beyond thinking about the safety and health of the animal. While trying to catch it quickly, it is possible to put the animals into situations like injuries and killing. Instead, it is aimed to catch animals safely with the "Harmful Wilderness Gate" which is a project that has more modern and more technological infrastructure, equipment and software combined.

With the remote control structure, it is planned that after the capture phase of the animal, the users will have a detailed knowledge about the trap and the animal in a safe structure. This information is to know the weight of the animal, its appearance and its position in case of multiple traps.
